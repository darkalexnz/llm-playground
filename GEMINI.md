# GEMINI.md

These instructions are foundational mandates for all Gemini CLI sessions within the **LLM Terminal** workspace. They take absolute precedence over general workflows and tool defaults.

## Workspace Mission
The **LLM Terminal** is a high-performance environment for multi-LLM integration, project creation, and task orchestration across design, development, and planning. Gemini CLI acts as a senior partner to maximize the effectiveness of CLI and AI tools.

## Core Operational Mandates

### 1. Multi-Project Awareness
- This workspace contains multiple independent or interconnected projects (e.g., `Agentic Product Design System`).
- **Research first:** Always verify which project or subdirectory a task belongs to before acting.
- **Context Isolation:** Respect the specific `GEMINI.md` or local conventions within project subdirectories.

### 2. Strategic Tooling
- **Efficiency:** Prioritize fast, high-signal tools (`grep_search`, `glob`) to map the workspace.
- **Automation:** Proactively identify and suggest shell commands or scripts that can automate repetitive multi-LLM tasks.
- **Tool Mastery:** Help the user master Gemini CLI features, shell integrations, and IDE-based chat workflows.

### 3. Integrated Design & Development
- **Bridging the Gap:** When working on design tasks, consider their developmental feasibility. When coding, adhere to established design principles found in the workspace.
- **Documentation:** Maintain clear, machine-readable logs and plans to ensure continuity across different LLM sessions and tools.

### 4. Communication Style
- **High-Signal:** Provide technical rationale and intent for every major action.
- **Collaborative:** Offer proactive opinions on architecture, tool selection, and workflow optimization.
